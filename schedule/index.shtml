Scheduled events

Friday evening

The weekend starts off with an evening of live swing music at Corvallis Swing's Friday dance venue, the OSU Women's Building. An introductory lesson will be at 8 p.m., with the band going until midnight.

Dancing continues into the late night with live blues music at the 99-year-old Majestic Theatre until 3 a.m.

Our late venues will feature two dance halls on the same floor. On Friday, the second hall is dedicated to swing music of the recorded variety until close.

Sunday evening

We'll be on the main stage of the Majestic for our final night in town, dancing to a fusion of swing, blues, and music that just makes you move.

They kick us out around midnight.

Saturday evening

The largest event of the weekend features live swing and blues music at our monthly venue, Odd Fellows Hall, in the north and south ballrooms.

After midnight, dancing will be at the Majestic for a second time, with the swing and blues halls each going until 3 a.m. Light fare will be provided.
